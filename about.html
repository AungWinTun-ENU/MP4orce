About Us

Our Mission

A small passionate developer and designer who lives up with one mission — to build a battle game that feels alive. We believe that even small indie projects deserve a level of polish that makes players feel something.

The journey started as a school project and quickly grew into something I genuinely care about.

Our Vision

Set out to create a turn-based battle experience that rewards strategy without sacrificing style. The game features four distinct heroes, each with their own personality, lore, and combat identity. Whether you prefer brute force, arcane precision, swift evasion, or tactical support — there's a hero for you.
