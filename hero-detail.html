Samkaith

Origins

Born from the shattered remnants of a dying star, Samkaith carries the fury of supernovae within her veins. Forged in the crucible of the Crimson Nebula, she emerged as a weapon of absolute destruction — a relentless force that leaves scorched earth in her wake. Her wrath is not born of hatred, but of an unbreakable will to protect what little remains of her shattered homeworld.

Combat Style

Samkaith overwhelms opponents with aggressive close-range assaults, combining devastating melee strikes with short-range plasma bursts. She excels at breaking enemy formations and absorbing punishment — the longer she fights, the more dangerous she becomes. Her critical hit rate spikes dramatically at low health, making her most lethal when cornered.

Dummling

Origins

Dummling was once a scholar of the Aetheric Archives, devoted to cataloguing the universe's hidden laws. After a catastrophic experiment tore open a rift between dimensions, he was merged with pure arcane energy — transforming him into a living conduit of raw power. Quiet and methodical, he approaches every battle like an equation waiting to be solved.

Combat Style

A precision-oriented mage who favors calculated, high-damage spells over brute force. Dummling reads the battlefield carefully before committing, punishing over-extensions with lethal magical volleys. His skill set rewards patience — setting up combination attacks that devastate single targets or exploit elemental weaknesses across the entire enemy team.

⚔️
Attack

Channels arcane bolt at a single target.

🌀

🔵

Absorbs next incoming hit. Lasts 1 turn.

✨

Bombards all enemies with arcane missiles.

Genji

Origins

Genji grew up in the Verdant Hollows — a hidden forest realm untouched by the wars that ravaged the outer worlds. Trained from birth in the ancient art of Windstep, she can move faster than the eye can follow. A lone wolf by nature, she wanders the fractured galaxy taking contracts, guided only by her own moral compass and an insatiable hunger for challenge.

Combat Style

Speed is Genji's greatest weapon. She darts between targets with fluid agility, landing multiple rapid strikes before enemies can react. She avoids direct confrontations, preferring hit-and-run tactics that whittle down foes over time. Her evasion rate is among the highest in the roster, and her poison abilities punish passive defensive play.

⚔️
Attack

Swift dual-blade strike on one enemy.

🍃

Dashes through target, dealing swift damage.

🐍

Poisons enemy. Deals damage each turn.

💨
Phantom Flurry

Hits random enemies 4–6 times rapidly.

KK

Origins

KK is an enigma. Records of her past were deliberately erased from every database across the known galaxy — even her own memory was wiped clean. What remains is a warrior of extraordinary instinct, operating purely on reflex and an uncanny ability to predict enemy movements. Some believe she was once a top-tier intelligence operative. KK neither confirms nor denies it.

Combat Style

KK fights with unpredictable, adaptive tactics that keep enemies constantly off-balance. She exploits every opening and punishes telegraphed attacks with precision counters. Her toolkit is a mix of support, disruption, and burst damage — making her the most versatile pick on the roster. In a coordinated team, KK elevates every ally around her.

Attack

Precise strike targeting enemy weak points.

⚡

Discharges energy — stuns target 1 turn.

🛡️

Takes hit in place of weakest ally.

🌩️

Boosts entire team's ATK for 2 turns.
